1 '''
2 NucleotideBF (nBF) Interpreter
3 Date created: 15th August 2012
4 Licence: Python Software Foundation License version 2
5 
6 NucleotideBF is a derivative of Brainfuck based on IUPAC
7 nucleotide code. It uses only 5 of the 8 operations in
8 Brainfuck, and there is no loop operations.
9 
10 The commands for nBF can be divided into 2 classes -
11 deterministic operations and random operations. The
12 deterministic operations are A (increment, equivalent to '+'),
13 T (decrement, equivalent to '-'), C (backward, equivalent to
14 '<'), G (forward, equivalent to '>') and '.' (call out).
15 
16 Based on the same interpreter as Loose Circular Brainfuck (LCBF),
17 the tape or array is circular (a ring list) instead of linear.
18 When the pointer is at the "end" of the tape, an increment ("A")
19 will move the tape to the start. Similarly, when the pointer is
20 decremented at the "beginning" of the tape, the pointer goes to
21 the end.
22 
23 @see: http://esolangs.org/wiki/NucleotideBF_(nBF)
24 '''
